# Inner Quest

This repo is a collection of prompts and tools for the [Inner Quest: Innovate Your Career Strategy](https://maven.com/james-gray/inner-quest) cohort course.

## Overview

Inner Quest is a 5-week cohort-based program that accelerates and transforms your career strategy through a powerful principle: **the key to career acceleration is to look inward first.** By combining internal self-discovery with external strategy development, you gain the clarity and decisive focus needed to stand out in your field.

## The Framework

The Inner Quest framework guides you through five transformative weeks:

1. **Discover Your Core** - Clarify your values, vision, and purpose
2. **Design Your Competitive Career Strategy** - Craft positioning aligned with market needs and personal strengths
3. **Embody Your Created Identity** - Build the mindset, behaviors, and habits of your future self
4. **Cultivate Self-Leadership** - Develop emotional resilience and focus on what you can control
5. **Manifest Your Vision** - Build your personal brand and activate your career offerings

## Philosophy

Inner Quest operates on the belief that true career transformation happens when you:
- Start with self-discovery before external strategy
- Develop a unique value proposition based on authentic strengths
- Build competitive positioning through portfolio career strategy
- Focus on controllable factors and emotional resilience
- Take intentional action aligned with your core values

## Outcomes

Participants achieve measurable results including increased compensation, enhanced self-confidence, standout personal branding, reduced career anxiety, and breakthrough performance.
